Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana (PM-KISAN)

Under this scheme, farmers with up to 2 hectares of land receive a direct cash transfer of Rs. 6000 per annum..
